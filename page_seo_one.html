LBEF College – The First IT College of Nepal
Setting the Benchmark for IT Education in Nepal

When it comes to pursuing higher education in the field of Information Technology in Nepal, students and parents often search for the Best IT College in Nepal. Since its establishment in 1998, Lord Buddha Education Foundation (LBEF College) has been recognized as a pioneer and is proudly known as the First IT College of Nepal. With more than 25 years of academic excellence, LBEF continues to shape future-ready graduates who are equipped to succeed in today’s technology-driven world.

Academic Excellence and Global Recognition

LBEF College offers a wide range of Undergraduate and Postgraduate programs in IT and Management, in collaboration with the world-ranked Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU), which is ranked among the Top 2% of universities worldwide. APU holds the distinction of being the only university rated QS 5★ Plus and accredited by the UK’s Quality Assurance Agency (QAA).

All programs are designed to provide students with a globally recognized qualification, ensuring their competitiveness in both local and international job markets. The college places strong emphasis on Outcome-Based Education (OBE), aligning teaching and learning with industry requirements. This approach ensures that graduates from LBEF are not only academically strong but also well-prepared for professional careers in IT.

Specialized IT Programs for the Future

In response to global trends and the evolving demands of the IT industry, LBEF offers B.Sc. IT (Hons.) programmes with futuristic specializations, including:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Cloud Engineering
  • Cyber Security
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Core IT

These specialized tracks enable students to build expertise in some of the most sought-after domains in today’s technology sector.

World-Class Infrastructure and Learning Environment

At LBEF College, students benefit from a modern learning ecosystem that includes:

  • Fully equipped computer laboratories and smart classrooms
  • Access to advanced software and tools
  • Guidance from highly qualified faculty members and industry professionals
  • Opportunities for research, innovation, and project-based learning

The institution also places a strong emphasis on extracurricular engagement through student clubs dedicated to AI, Robotics, Cyber Security, and Leadership Development, ensuring holistic growth beyond academics.

Scholarships and Financial Support

LBEF believes in making quality education accessible to deserving students. The college offers a wide range of scholarship opportunities based on academic merit, performance, and need. This ensures that talented students from diverse backgrounds can pursue their higher education at one of the Top IT Colleges in Nepal without financial barriers.

Credit Transfer Opportunities

To provide students with global exposure, LBEF offers flexible credit transfer pathways. Students can start their studies at LBEF Campus in Nepal and transfer credits to leading universities in:

  • United Kingdom (UK)
  • United States of America (USA)
  • Australia
  • Malaysia
  • Dubai
  • and many more countries

This global transfer facility allows students to complete part of their degree in Nepal and the remaining abroad, gaining international exposure and opportunities.

Strong Alumni Network and Industry Linkages

Over the years, LBEF has successfully produced more than 14,000 graduates, many of whom hold prominent positions in leading national and international organizations. With over 1,000 current students, the campus fosters a dynamic learning community supported by an extensive alumni network, creating valuable professional connections for its students.

Additionally, partnerships with the IT industry ensure students receive practical exposure through internships, workshops, seminars, and placement opportunities, bridging the gap between academic learning and workplace requirements.

Why LBEF is the Best IT College in Nepal

Students and parents searching for the Top IT Colleges in Nepal consistently recognize LBEF for its:

  • Legacy as the First IT College of Nepal
  • Internationally accredited programs
  • Affordable fee structure with scholarship opportunities
  • Focus on employability and industry readiness
  • Proven track record of successful graduates
